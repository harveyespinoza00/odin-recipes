Chicken Burritos

This is a chicken burrito made of lettuce, tomatoes, cheese and Tortilla

These are the Ingredients needed

  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Tortilla

This is how you make the burrito step by step

  1. Cook the chick and chop or shred it
  2. Add the Chicken to the tortilla
  3. Add the Lettuce and Tomatoes
  4. Add the Cheese
  5. Wrap all of the ingridents together
  6. Heat up to melt the cheese

You should have a finished Chicken Burrito!
