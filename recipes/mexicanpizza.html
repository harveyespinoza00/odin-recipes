Mexican Pizza

This is a Mexican Pizza, A dish of Ground Beef, Tortillas and More.

The Ingredients needed are as follows

  • Ground Beef
  • Tortilla
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Shredded Cheese

These are the steps to making the dish

  1. Cook the ground beef
  2. Slightly Fry the two tortillas
  3. Add the tomato sauce to the top of the first tortilla
  4. Add the ground beef
  5. Add the shredded Cheese
  6. Add a second tortilla and top with Tomato Sauce, Slightly less Beef and Cheese
  7. Heat the two tortillas up to melt the cheese

You should now have a finished Mexican Pizza Dish
