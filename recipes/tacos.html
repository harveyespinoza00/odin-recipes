Mexican Tacos

This dish is a Mexican dish consiting of beef, tortillas, onion and Cilantro

The Ingredients needed are as follows

  • Beef of choice
  • Tortillas
  • Onions
  • Cilantro

The steps needed to make the dish are as follows

  1. Cook the beef of choice and cut it up
  2. Add the beef to the tortilla
  3. Cut the onions and Cilantro
  4. Add the onion and Cilantro to tortilla

You should have a finihsed Mexican Taco dish!
